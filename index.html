My Approach

How I (typically) Work
Step 01

Exploration & Insight

I begin by understanding the challenge in context—space sector dynamics, sustainability implications, digital maturity, and organisational realities. This discovery phase helps surface real constraints, opportunities, and strategic levers.

Step 02

Strategic Mapping

Using insights gathered, I shape a clear direction—whether that’s a sustainability pathway, a digital growth strategy, a space-sector market position, or a communications framework. The outputs are structured, evidence-based, and actionable.

Step 03

Implementation & Iteration

From developing campaigns and frameworks to building digital systems and impact models, I work collaboratively to turn strategy into practice. I prioritise agility, transparency, and measurable progress throughout delivery.

Step 04

Review, Learn & Evolve

Once work is live, I help organisations embed learning, refine approaches, and scale effectively. Whether optimising a sustainability initiative or enhancing digital systems, I ensure long-term resilience and momentum.

Have Questions? Explore My Insights

Here are answers to some common questions about my work in space innovation, sustainability, and digital strategy. If your question isn’t listed, feel free to reach out directly.

What services do you offer for space startups?

I provide strategic consulting, market analysis, and sustainable growth planning for space-focused organisations, helping them align operations with industry best practices and environmental responsibility.

How do you integrate sustainability into digital strategy?

I help organisations implement data-driven marketing and operational strategies that reduce environmental impact, optimise resource usage, and drive measurable sustainability outcomes.

Do you work with policy makers or only private companies?

My work spans startups, established companies, and public sector actors. I provide insights, frameworks, and guidance that support responsible innovation and informed decision-making across the space ecosystem.

Can you help with digital transformation projects?

Yes. I specialise in designing and implementing digital strategies, leveraging analytics, automation, and technology solutions to enhance efficiency, engagement, and impact for organisations in multiple sectors.

Our Approach

How Impact Orbit Group Delivers Value
Step 01

Discovery & Insight

We start by understanding your challenges, context, and goals across the space ecosystem. This includes sector trends, sustainability opportunities, digital maturity, stakeholder needs, and organisational realities. Insight drives clarity and identifies strategic levers for impact.

Step 02

Strategy & Design

Building on insights, we co-create strategies tailored to your objectives—whether that’s digital transformation via Green Orbit Digital, communications and PR campaigns through Green Orbit Space Communications & PR, capability development via Green Orbit Academy, or event-led engagement like Space Impact Forum and Leicester Space Week. Our outputs are structured, evidence-based, and actionable.

Step 03

Implementation & Collaboration

We translate strategy into action—developing campaigns, digital systems, communications frameworks, impact models, and events. Across all ventures, collaboration, agility, and measurable progress ensure your organisation achieves tangible outcomes and long-term impact.

Step 04

Review, Learn & Scale

We embed learning, optimise approaches, and scale solutions across your initiatives. From sustainability programmes to digital platforms and public engagement events, our focus is on resilience, continuous improvement, and creating lasting positive impact throughout the space ecosystem.

Have Questions? Explore Our Insights

Here are answers to some common questions about our work across the Impact Orbit Group ecosystem, including Green Orbit Digital, Green Orbit Space Communications & PR, Green Orbit Academy, Space Impact Forum, Space Integrity Initiative, and Leicester Space Week. If your question isn’t listed, feel free to reach out directly.

What services does Impact Orbit Group provide?

We offer strategic consulting, communications, digital strategy, capability development, and sustainability guidance across our ventures. Our services support space organisations, startups, and community initiatives in delivering impact responsibly and effectively.

How do you integrate sustainability across your projects?

We embed sustainability into digital strategy, communications, events, and innovation projects. This includes ESG-aligned frameworks, low-carbon practices, responsible governance, and measurement of social and environmental outcomes across the space sector.

Do you collaborate with government, academia, or only private organisations?

Our work spans the full ecosystem: startups, established companies, public sector bodies, academic institutions, and cross-sector initiatives. We provide guidance and frameworks to enable responsible innovation and informed decision-making across the space and sustainability sectors.

Can you support digital transformation or capability development?

Yes. Through Green Orbit Digital and Green Orbit Academy, we deliver digital transformation programmes, analytics-driven strategies, automation solutions, and training workshops that enhance organisational efficiency, engagement, and impact.

How can I engage with your initiatives and events?

You can connect via our contact form or subscribe for updates. We also host the Space Impact Forum, Leicester Space Week, collaborative labs, workshops, and public engagement programmes, providing opportunities for networking, learning, and contributing to sustainable space innovation.

Can you help with communications and public engagement?

Through Green Orbit Space Communications & PR, we support storytelling, media engagement, and public campaigns that clearly convey complex space and sustainability initiatives to stakeholders, media, and wider audiences.
